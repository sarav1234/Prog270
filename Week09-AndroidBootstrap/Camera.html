Camera Application

This application comes preloaded on the Galaxy S4 and is the default camera application. The device has 2 cameras:

  • Rear-facing
    • Resolution: 13 Megapixels
    • 4X digital zoom

  • Front-facing
    • Resolution: 2 Megapixels

Modes


Beauty Face

The camera will detect the face and show the enhanced face in real time. Pretty much, it evens out the skin tone of the face. The directions also say that this may not work when taking close up pictures.

Best Face

Select the best picture of each person from 5 consecutive pictures to get the best merged group picture.

Eraser

This mode saves the best picture after erasing moving objects from 5 consecutive (burst) pictures.
