Galaxy S4 pages




Settings

The Settings can be accessed from the Application screen. The settings are separated into 4 sections:

  • Connections. Here you can set up Wifi, Bluetooth, NFC, and more.
  • My device. Here you can personalize your display.
  • Accounts. The accounts you added to the device and backup options are here
  • More. Here you can set up permissions and system manager options.



Recent Apps

The page shows the recently used applications. You can click on an image to return to the application, close 1 or all applications, and access the Task Manager (icon at bottom left).

Social Networking




Twitter

Using this application, you can follow other Twitter users; and tweet (post a short message) on your wall or someone else's.



Facebook

This is another networking application. You can update your status, post pictures and links, and read your friend's posts. Each user has a page with a "wall" where various posts go.



Google+

Google + is sort of like Facebook. You add people to your "circles", you can post messages and images, and view your friends' posts.

Data Storage Apps




Memo App

This application came already installed on the device. You can create various notes, either using the virtual keyboard, or writing on the screen with your finger or stylus. You can also attach images or password protect some notes.



Evernote App

This application was downloaded from the PlayStore. It essentually does the same as Note except that your data is stored on the cloud so you can access and edit it from your mobile device, your desktop, or online.



Google Drive App

This application is accessible from the mobile application or through any browser. It includes 15GB of free storage. You can upload, download,and edit any file stored in Google Drive. You can also share a file or folder with anyone with an email address.
