Things To Do

At Clear Lake, there are a lot of things to do if you enjoy spending time outdoors. First of all, there are several beautiful campgrounds. I highly recommend going there during the summer. While enjoying the natural and sleeping under the stars, you can go fishing. The following fish are found at Clear Lake.

Fishing

Brown Bullhead     
Ictalurus Catus     
Largemouth Bass     
Channel Catfish     


Hiking

You can also go hiking. There's a road that goes all around Clear Lake; and at some point, the road goes over a river and it is the most georgous sight. The image below gives you a peek into this hidden treasure. It was taken early in the morning as the fog starts to thin out. There are a few places when you can rent a kayak or canoe. We did a lot of swimming. The water is not too cold and there are several places where there is sand on the bottom.
