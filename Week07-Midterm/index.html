Clear Lake

I went camping to Clear Lake for the first time this summer. We were looking for somewhere near water between Richland, WA and Bonney Lake, WA. It takes about 2.5 hrs to drive there. The address and a map are located on our Address page.

Clear Lake is not only somewhere to go camping. You can also go fishing, swimming, and kayaking. For more information on things to do, please see our Activities page.



View of Clear Lake Early Morning

Clear Lake is just one of my favorite places to go. It's a quick drive and it is very peaseful. And who can resist smores! I would love to retire somewhere like this.
